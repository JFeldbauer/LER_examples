Daily meteorological data are from an on-lake weather station located at a nearby lake (16.6 km from Lake Giles), as described in
Pilla & Couture (in review). These full data can be accessed at: https://www.lehigh.edu/~brh0/pocono_mon/index.html.

Lake temperature profile data and additional details about Lake Giles can be found at:
Williamson, C.E. 2020. Three decades of limnological data from lakes in the Pocono Mountains region, Pennsylvania USA, 1988-2019
ver 3. Environmental Data Initiative. https://doi.org/10.6073/pasta/11b0c410adad22290b74522a4321c271.
